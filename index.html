Childhood

I cannot tell you too many things about me as a child. Just that I was crazy for sweets. Growing up

Growing up, I've always liked math better than literature, maybe because when I was in the elementery school my mom "tortured" me in weekends by doing math problems.

Because I was always organized and detail-oriented, it felt like a natural choice to go to Economic High School and then to study Finance and Banks at Transilvania Univerity.

Right after finnishing the studies, I started working in Bancpost. I worked there for 3 years and then I moved to Banca Transilvania, where I've worked for another 3 years. I've learned many things but what made me unhappy was the tremendous pressure which was not justified. Although I am grateful for what I've learned, when I'd left I was like

Nowadays, I work in a totally diferent domain and I am happier.
